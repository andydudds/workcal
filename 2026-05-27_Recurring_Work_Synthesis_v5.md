### Monthly close (per Finance Ops Guide + Month-End Closing Process v2)

Two date conventions in the official process. Early internal close uses business days. FinCom-facing distribution uses calendar dates with a weekend/holiday shift to the prior business day. Both shown explicitly below.

| Date | Anchor type | Block | Time | What |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Biz day 4 | Biz day | Close health check | 15 min | Early warning, recoverable now |
| Biz day 5 | Biz day | Ramp final sync | 45 min | Expenses submitted, Ramp → QB sync clean (Pat's lift, now Andy's) |

| Biz day 12 | Biz day | Revenue recon checkpoint | 30 min | With Elizabeth + Juliana on SF-QBO alignment |
| 15th | Calendar | Cash co-verify | 45 min | With Souley. Don't share figure until both sign off |
| 16th-17th | Calendar | Books closed review | 90 min | Statement review before slides |
| 17th | Calendar (official) | Books closed, statements generated | (deliverable) | Per Finance Ops Guide |
| 18th-19th | Calendar | Slide build (deep work, do-not-schedule) | 3 hrs | Inherited from Pat. Highest-risk block. Includes Performance Highlights at a Glance and co-signed cash |
| 19th | Calendar (official) | Draft review slides sent to ELT | (deliverable) | Per Finance Ops Guide |
| 20th-21st | Calendar | ELT comment integration | 45 min | Incorporate, recirculate if needed |

Weekend/holiday rule: if any deliverable date falls on a weekend or holiday, it shifts to the prior business day. Monthly results distribute by the 23rd every month regardless of whether a FinCom meeting is scheduled.

## Section 7: Reality Check (Hours Math)

### Baseline weekly load (steady state, no peak window)

| Bucket | Hours/wk |
|---|---|
| Recurring meetings (post v3 cleanup) | 7.1 |
| New recurring blocks (weekly + monthly prorated) | 2.9 |
| Monthly close (prorated) | 2.3 |
| Direct report development (Amanda monthly skip + quarterly dev prorated) | 0.4 |
| Tim workstreams (mid estimate) | 5.0 |
| Weekly Avanan security review | 0.5 |
| Email (1 hr/day Mon-Fri) | 5.0 |
| Ad-hoc reviews, fire drills, other interruptions | 3.5 |
| **Baseline subtotal** | **~25.2 hrs/wk** |

Leaves ~14.8 hrs/wk in a 40 hour week. Honest, tight, but workable in steady state.

### Peak window add-ons (additive to baseline)

| Window | Add-on hours/wk in window |
|---|---|
| Apr-Jun (audit + insurance + board + 990 start) | +6 to +10 |
| Jul-Aug (workers comp + OE + retreat + WI filing) | +5 to +8 |
| Nov-Dec (990 + ancillary OE + laptops + M365 + budget wrap) | +6 to +10 |

In peak windows, structural commitments alone hit 31-35 hrs/wk. Discretionary capacity for new asks drops to 5-9 hrs/wk.

**Operating implication:** in peak windows, "no" or "later" needs to be the default answer to new asks, not the exception.
